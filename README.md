# Economics, Finance, Capital Markets, and Strategic Analysis

This repository documents my learning projects, analytical work, and financial modeling practice across **economics, finance, capital markets, investment analysis, and strategic decision-making**.

The main purpose of this repository is to build a structured portfolio that connects financial theory with real-world business, investment, and corporate decision problems.

## Repository Overview

This repository is organized around five core areas:

```text
Economics-Finance-Analytics/
├── 01-Engineering-Economy-and-Feasibility/
├── 02-Financial-Modeling-and-Valuation/
├── 03-Investment-Banking-and-Private-Equity/
├── 04-CFA-and-Capital-Markets/
├── 05-Macroeconomics-and-Strategy/
└── README.md
```

Each folder represents a major domain in finance and economics. The structure is designed to support a long-term learning path toward roles in corporate finance, investment banking, private equity, capital markets, financial analysis, and strategic business decision-making.

## Folder Description

### 01 — Engineering Economy and Feasibility

This folder contains projects and notes related to engineering economy, investment feasibility, and capital budgeting.

Main topics include:

* Net Present Value
* Internal Rate of Return
* Payback Period
* Equivalent Annual Cost
* Cost-benefit analysis
* Replacement analysis
* Project feasibility study
* Sensitivity and scenario analysis

This section connects engineering decision-making with financial evaluation.

### 02 — Financial Modeling and Valuation

This folder focuses on building financial models to analyze businesses, projects, and investment opportunities.

Main topics include:

* Three-statement financial model
* Discounted Cash Flow valuation
* Revenue and cost modeling
* Unit economics
* Startup financial model
* Sensitivity analysis
* Scenario analysis
* Business valuation

This section is designed to develop practical financial modeling skills using spreadsheets and analytical reasoning.

### 03 — Investment Banking and Private Equity

This folder contains materials and projects related to deal analysis, corporate transactions, and investment decision-making.

Main topics include:

* Investment banking fundamentals
* Mergers and acquisitions
* Comparable company analysis
* Precedent transaction analysis
* Leveraged Buyout analysis
* Private equity investment thesis
* Due diligence framework
* Pitchbook-style analysis

This section is focused on understanding how financial institutions evaluate companies, structure transactions, and create investment value.

### 04 — CFA and Capital Markets

This folder contains structured learning notes and practice related to capital markets and CFA-style financial analysis.

Main topics include:

* Ethics and professional standards
* Quantitative methods
* Economics
* Financial statement analysis
* Corporate issuers
* Equity investments
* Fixed income
* Derivatives
* Alternative investments
* Portfolio management
* Capital market analysis

This section is designed as a long-term foundation for disciplined financial analysis and investment decision-making.

### 05 — Macroeconomics and Strategy

This folder connects macroeconomic analysis with business and investment strategy.

Main topics include:

* GDP and economic growth
* Inflation and interest rates
* Monetary policy
* Fiscal policy
* Exchange rates
* Business cycle analysis
* Country and industry analysis
* Market cycle interpretation
* Strategic economic outlook

This section helps translate macroeconomic conditions into practical business and investment implications.
